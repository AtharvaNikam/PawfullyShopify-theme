🌱 our story

It started with a
stubborn beagle.

In 2014, our founder's beagle Biscuit refused every treat on the shelf. Faced with a very judgemental 14kg critic, she got to work in her kitchen. One salmon-forward recipe and 10,000 zoomies later, Paws & Co. was born.

A decade on, we're a small team obsessed with making pet products that don't suck — stuff you'd actually be proud to have on your counter, that your pet is even more excited to see.

Everything we sell is vet-approved, cruelty-free, and small-batch. Because your weird little gremlin deserves more than landfill-grade squeakers.

Woof yeah!
⚡ what we stand for

Three things we
won't budge on

🌿

Real ingredients

If we wouldn't feed it to our own pets, it's not going on our shelf. Real food, identifiable first. No mystery meats, no filler fluff.

💛

Tail-tested

Every single product goes through our in-house panel of 47 dogs, 23 cats, and 3 very opinionated rabbits before it hits the shop.

🌍

Better for the planet

Compostable packaging, carbon-neutral shipping, and 1% of every sale goes to local shelters. Small steps, big tails.
